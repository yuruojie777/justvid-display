About justvid

justvid is an AI-powered video creation tool for global creators.

justvid focuses on prompt-based video generation, flexible model selection, project history, and fast iteration for creative workflows. It is designed to be simple enough for beginners while powerful enough for professional creators.

  • Multiple AI video models (e.g. WAN 2.5 and more)
  • Simple and fast prompt-based video creation
  • Project history & export options
  • Basic safety & reporting mechanisms
